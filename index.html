An Introduction

Curiosity has always been a driving factor in my education. I enjoy asking questions, and upon coming to UC Berkeley I discovered data science - a rising field encouraging this curiosity. I'm fascinated by how human brains guide our complicated decision-making abilities, and transferring that knowledge to the computer science domain, where we teach machines to make better decisions. I'd like to use my constant curiosity to drive innovation by asking the challenging questions and using data to create meaningful answers. As a lead researcher and student instructor within data science at Berkeley, it's absolutely rewarding to inspire other students to also drive innovation through curiosity and data-driven decision making. I'm interested in the fields of data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.
