An Introduction

Curiosity has always been a driving factor in my education. I enjoy asking questions, and upon coming to UC Berkeley I discovered data science - a rising field encouraging this curiosity. I'm fascinated by how human brains guide our complicated decision-making abilities, and transferring that knowledge to the computer science domain, where we can teach machines to make better decisions. I'd like to drive innovation by asking the challenging questions and using data to create meaningful answers. As a student researcher and instructor at Berkeley, it's rewarding to inspire other students to drive innovation through data-driven decision making. I'm interested in the fields of AI, software engineering, data science, and machine learning.
